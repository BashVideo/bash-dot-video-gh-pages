Bash

It's been a blast...

We’re thrilled to announce that the team here at Bash Video is joining Roblox, a global platform bringing millions of people together through shared experiences.

When we started Bash in 2020, we recognized the profound need for people to stay connected, share experiences and have fun in new ways – and we built our video conferencing application focused on social gathering. Now, we are moving to the next phase of our journey. We believe the pandemic has permanently shaped the future of human-to-human interactions and behaviors.

Roblox has a compelling vision to build a UGC platform where billions of people can come together to play, learn, work and share experiences in immersive 3D environments. Key to this is empowering its developer community around the world to reach a massive audience, build their skills, and be rewarded for their creativity.

Before Bash, our team helped build Kongregate – one of the first platforms that enabled indie developers to do all of these things. Our team will be joining the Developer Group at Roblox to share our collective entrepreneurial, engineering and product development expertise to benefit the growing Roblox development community.

And we’re not the only ones who are excited. Our kids and their friends are too! They are playing on Roblox, and learning to build their own experiences. The chance to help the next generation of creators learn to code and collaborate speaks to us.

Finally, we’d like to thank everyone who has supported us to get here - our users, our families, and our investors, led by Bloomberg Beta and Precursor Ventures.
